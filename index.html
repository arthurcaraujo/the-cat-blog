Cats that scratch furniture are often marking their territory

Cats have tiny scent glands on their feet, and when they scratch furniture they leave just a little bit of their smell on it. This tells other cats, “This sofa is in my territory, and these are my people—keep away!”

Plant-based diets may be healthier for cats

A study published in the scientific journal PLOS ONE reveals that cats fed on a plant based diet were found to be generally more healthy than those on a meat diet. The study was based on a survey of 1,369 cat owners or “guardians” who fed plant-based or meat-based diets to their cats for at least one year.

Discouraging biting

Play biting is natural kitten behavior. If your kitten bites your hand during play, end the session immediately. Whatever you do, don't jerk your hand away. This just makes the game even more fun! Toys and balls are safer objects of prey.
